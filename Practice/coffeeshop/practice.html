Our Story

This story begins when I met my wife, Shelby. Up until then it was quite rare that you would find me around a coffee shop let alond drinking it.

That is until one day, on my first visit to Texas, I had to wake up at 6 o'clock in the morning to drive her to work. Seeing as I was still a college student; this was no easy task. This is when I got the first taste of coffee in my adult life.
